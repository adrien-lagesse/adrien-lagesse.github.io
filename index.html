About me

I am a born and raised Mauritian. After two years studying Mathematics and Physics at Lycée Louis Le Grand in Paris, I specialised in Mathematics at École Polytechnique. I am interested in Representation Learning and the Mathematical structures that arise in Deep Learning. I have worked on several projects in Natural Language Processing, Finance and Computer Vision.

I will be attending Imperial College London for the 2022-2023 year where I will undertake a Master in Fundamental Mathematics.
